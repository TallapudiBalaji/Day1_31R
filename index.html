Heading Tags: HTML heading tags are used to define headings in a webpage and structure content hierarchically. There are six levels of heading tags, from h1 to h6, with h1 being the most important and h6 the least. These tags help improve readability, accessibility, and SEO by indicating the importance and organization of the content.
